Eco-Friendly Habits

Small changes for a sustainable future

üåø Everyday Habits

Reduce, Reuse, Recycle

  • Cut down on waste by choosing reusable items.
  • Recycle paper, plastic, glass, and metals properly.

Use Less Plastic

  • Say no to single-use plastics like straws, bags, and bottles.
  • Opt for cloth bags, metal straws, and glass or steel containers.

Save Water

  • Fix leaks and turn off taps when not in use.
  • Use water-efficient appliances.

Save Energy

  • Switch off lights and electronics when not needed.
  • Use energy-efficient bulbs and appliances.

üåç Community & Lifestyle

Plant Trees & Support Green Spaces

  • Trees clean the air and provide shelter for wildlife.
  • Volunteer for local clean-up or tree planting events.

Use Eco-Friendly Transportation

  • Walk, bike, carpool, or use public transport to reduce emissions.
  • Consider electric or hybrid vehicles if possible.

Support Sustainable Brands

  • Choose products from companies that prioritize the environment.
  • Look for eco-certifications and ethical practices.

Avoid Littering & Pick Up Trash

  • Always dispose of waste properly.
  • Carry a small trash bag when hiking or at the beach.

Educate Others

  • Share what you know about environmental protection.
  • Encourage friends and family to adopt eco-friendly habits.

Advocate for Change

  • Support environmental policies and leaders.
  • Join groups or movements focused on sustainability.
